Getting started

Here’s how to get rolling:

  1. Create a new "Sherlock"

    Select all the features you're looking for in an apartment

  2. PadIntel uses your Sherlock to perpetually crawl craigslist for matches.

    Sherlocks are smart enough to search for multiple ways to say the same thing.

    For example, if you want carpet, it looks for posts saying "carpet" or "carpeting" or "wall-to-wall..."

  3. Find your new place

    Sort through the chaos and find exactly what you're looking for with PadIntel.
